About Us

Mediprime Services (Medical Equipments Sell & Services) is the ISO 9001: 2015 (Quality Management System) & QCAS-2019-MPES-1029 (Compliance department) Certified Company based in Jharkhand, India.

Fully specialized in services and consulting for Medical equipment sell, service & management. Mediprime Services activities will be dedicated to the medical sector in its large scale and the company will offer an all-inclusive service going from the problem identification to the solution application based on fast analysis and consulting. Our objective is to provide exceptional service while providing healthcare buyers access to services and a comprehensive portfolio of products across several trusted manufacturers.

This is done with a view of introducing new product ranges as well as to strengthen ties. Company has maintained a standard policy of keeping the quality universal for all customers. Unlike some of our competitors here in India who chose to neglect quality while distributing goods especially to developing nations.
